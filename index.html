How to use

Drag the FontPeek button to your bookmarks bar (or save the code as a bookmark URL). On any website, click the bookmark to turn on the inspector. Hover over text to see its font details; click to lock the panel and hit Copy CSS. Click elsewhere to unlock. Press Esc to exit.
